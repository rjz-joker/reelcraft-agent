      QUICK ANSWERS

      Frequently asked questions

      Does ReelCraft use real Shopify product data?＋

      Yes. Public Shopify product pages are parsed through the backend to retrieve listing data such as title, description, variants, pricing, and media. Review data requires a separate review provider.

      When does Seedance consume credits?＋

      Credits are consumed only after you confirm a premium generation and the request is submitted to the configured provider. Starter videos remain free in this MVP.

      Can ReelCraft publish directly to TikTok?＋

      The current MVP prepares and stores a complete publishing package. Direct posting requires TikTok OAuth and approval for the Content Posting API.
